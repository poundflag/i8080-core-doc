enum Condition

Defines different conditions for branching instructions.

Enumerator
COND_CARRY 

Carry flag is set.

COND_NO_CARRY 

Carry flag is not set.

COND_MINUS 

Negative (MSB of A is 1)

COND_POSITIVE 

Positive or zero (MSB of A is 0)

COND_PARITY_EVEN 

Parity flag is even.

COND_PARITY_ODD 

Parity flag is odd.

COND_ZERO 

Zero flag is set.

COND_NOT_ZERO 

Zero flag is not set.
